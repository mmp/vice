
Since IRL uses MEARTS and not STARS some conversions have been made. Handoffs to Center do not use the standard "C" or other letter and slew. Instead, the closest approximation to what is done in MEARTS can be accomplished just like handing off to an adjacent STARS facility in STARS:  `<TRIANGLE> <TCP> <SLEW>`. (Ex: `<TRIANGLE> J <SLEW>` for a handoff to HCF Sector 2)
